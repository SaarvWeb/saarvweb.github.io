Focus Areas: Education | SMEs & Innovation

ABOUT US

SAARV is a Finland-based consultancy supporting collaboration between Europe and India. We support universities, polytechnics, schools, startups, innovative clusters, and SMEs to build structured and effective cross-border partnerships.

Our approach is simple: Regulatory clarity. Policy alignment. Cultural intelligence. Ethical execution.

We ensure that every collaboration — academic or commercial — is compliant with EU regulatory frameworks, Indian compliance systems, and international best practices.
