ABOUT US

SAARV is a Finland-based consultancy bridging European structure with Indian dynamism. We specialize in guiding universities,applied science institutes, startups, innovative clusters, and SMEs in building credible and effective structure with India.

Our approach is simple: Legal clarity. Policy alignment. Cultural intelligence. Ethical execution.

We ensure that every collaboration — academic or commercial — is compliant with EU regulations, Indian laws, and international best practices.
